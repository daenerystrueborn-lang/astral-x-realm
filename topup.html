Frequently asked

Solars are the primary in-game currency used to buy equipment, enter dungeons, and upgrade your character in Astral X Realm.
Gems are the premium currency for rare card pulls, exclusive cosmetics, and special season pass content. They're harder to earn through gameplay.
After a successful payment, your Solars and Gems are credited to your account within minutes. You'll receive a confirmation in your registered Discord or WhatsApp.
All purchases are final unless there was a delivery error on our end. Contact support in our Discord server within 48 hours of purchase.
Solars and Gems carry over between seasons. Season-specific cosmetics and pass rewards do not transfer, but are permanently unlocked in your collection.
